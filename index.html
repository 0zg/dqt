what is a Data Quant

A Data Quant fulfils the roles of a business analyst, a software developer and a data engineer.

By analyzing existing business processes we can determine where data-driven additions can optimize the business.

All three roles are needed to prevent data-driven products from becoming islands outside of the existing business.
